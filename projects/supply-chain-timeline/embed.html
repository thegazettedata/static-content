Links in the agricultural equipment supply chain

For the last month, reporter Brian Morelli has looked into how agriculture planters are built. For the last part of the series, Morelli visited Iowa farmers, who use the planters in their fields to sow corn and soy seeds, which will eventually grow to feed people and animals and fuel vehicles.
